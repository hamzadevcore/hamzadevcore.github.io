CRISTOL: DEEP DIVE

CRISTOL

"Live inside the stories you love"

Cristol is a next-generation AI Game Master and collaborative fiction platform built to solve the two biggest problems in long-term AI roleplay:

  • Context Collapse - AI forgets complex plot threads over time
  • Loss of Player Agency - AI starts speaking for the user

Uses custom engine with dynamic memory management for sagas spanning millions of words without degradation.

:: THE THREE LAWS ::

Law I: Strict Player Autonomy
AI acts as world/NPCs but forbidden from writing user's dialogue, thoughts, or actions.
Law II: Chunk Completion
AI focuses on rendering current immediate scene with full immersion.
Law III: The Director's Notebook
AI secretly logs relationships, NPC motivations, and plot hooks via [SECRET_NOTES].
> Context Chunking

Divides Blueprints into Chapters/Chunks. Rolling Continuity compresses history.

> World Blueprints

Web Crawler scrapes Wikis. Importer handles EPUBs/PDFs. Auto-Lore generates Voice Bibles.

> Multiverse Timelines

Visual node-graph. Branch from any point. Spawn alternate realities.

> Affinity Web

Interactive Relationship Web with Friendship/Romance scores and motivations.

> Oracle & Downtime

AI assistant suggests actions, proposes twists, helps generate OCs.

> Multiplayer Uplink

Secure sharing links. Read-Only or Full Collaboration modes.
